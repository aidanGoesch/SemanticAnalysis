A man waited at a bus stop, sweating through a red T-shirt, holding a plastic grocery bag with melting ice cream inside.
A black SUV screeched to a stop nearby.
A frantic woman rushed to a payphone, failed to make a call, and then begged to borrow the man’s phone.
She called someone on it, argued angrily into it, and smashed it onto the street.
The man protested and asked for some money for the phone as she broke down crying claiming another man is on the way.
Another man arrived under a bridge, blasting loud music.
The woman confronted him furiously, accusing him and demanding to know where someone was.
After the new man demands to know who he is, the first man awkwardly tried to explain he was just waiting for the bus.
Tensions escalated as the second man revealed himself as a magician and the woman as his assistant.
The woman pulled out a gun, screaming for answers.
The magician produced his own gun and began chanting from a strange relic.
The woman opens the magician's trunk and is shocked.
The first man collapsed as the spell took hold, barking and convulsing.
The woman, in anger, shoots the magician.
In response, the magician shoots the woman and they both collapse on the ground.
The man lay in melted ice cream, barking like a dog.
He crawled away on all fours, sniffing and howling.
Another woman appeared from the car, also crawling and barking.
They sniffed each other, played, and moved off together, no longer human but instead acting like dogs.
