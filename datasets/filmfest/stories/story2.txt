A woman stood alone in a room with a pizza box.
She opened the box and took out a slice.
A knock sounded at the door.
She opened it and found a record.
She placed the record on her player and lowered the needle.
Cheerful music began to play.
She swayed to the music and while lifting the pizza the record skipped, and part of the pizza vanished.
She noticed the change, stopped the record, and spun it backward.
The missing pizza returned to her hand.
She experimented by spinning the record back and forth and the pizza progressively vanished and reappeared.
She advances the record further, and gets teleported to a new house.
She is now older, both married and pregnant.
She spins the record and notices her pregnant belly grows and shrinks.
She advances the record some more and has her baby in her hand.
The baby starts crying, so she rewinds the record only to end up as a child now.
She struggles to reach the record, and advances it quite a bit and now is an elderly woman in a wheelchair.
She can no longer reach the record without time resetting her, so she falls over and the record goes even further.
She is now even more elderly, and as she walks over to the record player the record ends and only her ash jar remains.
