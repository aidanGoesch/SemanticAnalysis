A dirty black running shoe lay on a sidewalk.
The shoe turned upright as a foot tapped the ground.
A candy wrapper fell beside the shoe.
A milk carton was opened.
A man looked down and smiled slightly.
He licked his lips.
He peered into the carton and took a drink.
He glanced around and drank again.
Across the street, a woman walked in a red shirt and black skirt.
The man leaned against a maroon car.
Another man approached him in a confrontational way.
The man with the milk backed away.
He walked down the street, watching the woman.
He tripped on the curb but recovered.
The woman looked toward him, then stumbled while walking.
They began copying each other’s movements.
Their gestures grew playful and exaggerated.
The man stepped forward without noticing traffic.
A car struck him, sending his car and shoes flying through the air.
One of the shoes lands next to another black shoe.
