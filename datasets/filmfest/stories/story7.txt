A man in a black suit approached a maid cleaning a table.
He asked where he was, but her answers were vague.
Growing anxious, he asked for the police station.
She explained that there was none.
He asked to use a phone and was directed to a phone box.
Ominous music played as he walked through the village.
At the phone box, he tried to make a call.
The operator refused him because he had no number.
The man walked back through the street, scanning his surroundings.
He stopped at an information board and pressed a button.
A taxi appeared almost immediately.
The driver spoke casually as she drove him through the village.
They passed houses, fountains, and narrow roads.
She dropped him off near a white building and drove away.
The man entered a general store and asked for a map.
The clerk offered only maps of the village.
Every map showed the village enclosed by mountains and named Your Village.
Realizing he could not leave, the man exited the store.
