A tired man stood beside a copy machine and sighed.
He pressed a button, and the machine beeped repeatedly.
Annoyed, he kicked the copier.
The machine printed a solid black circle on white paper.
The man picked up the page and studied it.
He set it aside and checked if anything was in the copier but there was nothing.
He reached for his drink and placed it on the paper.
The cup vanished into the black circle.
Startled, the man leaned closer to inspect it.
He cautiously tested it with his hand.
To his surprise, he pulled his cup back out.
Curious, he experimented by retrieving candy from a vending machine.
The man smiled, encouraged by his discovery.
He noticed a locked door marked “Keep Out.”
Using the black hole, he unlocked the door from the inside.
He placed the paper on a safe and pulled out cash.
Greed took over as he climbed fully into the hole.
The paper fell away, leaving him trapped in the locked safe.
