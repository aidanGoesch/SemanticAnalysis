A woman stood at a printer, quietly making copies.
Across a thin border, a man did the same.
Both stood up at the same moment and noticed each other.
They quickly looked away, flustered and smiling, and returned to work.
The man sat at his desk and opened a blue folder.
Inside, he found a smiley face made of pink post-it notes.
He looked around, confused but amused.
The woman briefly peeked out from her cubicle and hid again.
The man smiled and thought for a moment.
At her desk, the woman opened the blinds.
A sun made of post-it notes filled her window.
She smiled happily and sat down.
Later, the man adjusted his tie in the bathroom mirror and noticed a heart made from post-it notes.
The woman gathered armfuls of post-it notes from a supply closet and hurried down the hallway
She saw the man making a giant post-it note mural of their faces on the wall.
The man turned to her, and they smiled at each other.
