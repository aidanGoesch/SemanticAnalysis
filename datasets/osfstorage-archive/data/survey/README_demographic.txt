demographic_pieman_eyespy.xlsx contains the basic demographic information and survey answers of all 116 participants. This set of participants listened to and recalled "pieman" and "eyespy".

Columns:
ID: participant ID

platform: data collection platform

age: participants' age as of time of collection

gender: participants' reported gender.

order_pieman: the order in which the story "pieman“  was presented. (1 is the first, 2 is the second)

order_eyespy: the order in which the story "eyespy“  was presented. (1 is the first, 2 is the second)

Slider_understood (from 1 to 5): slider response to the question "I understood the task instructions."

Slider_engaged (from 1 to 5): slider response to the question "I was engaged in the experiment. In other words, I payed close attention to the stories and took the task seriously."

Slider_difficult (from 1 to 5): slider response to the question "I found the experiment difficult."

Text_strategy: typed response to the question "Did you use a particular strategy to memorize the story (e.g., imagining yourself in the story or simply paying close attention)?"

Text_memorable: typed response to the question "Were there any moments in either story that you found particularly memorable? If so, please describe them below."

Text_shouldknow: typed response to the question "Is there anything you think we should know about your experience taking our experiment?"

Text_native: typed response to the question "Are you a native english speaker? That is, is english your first language?"

Text_otherlang: typed response to the question "Do you speak any other languages besides English? If so, how many years have you spoken this language(s) and what is your level of fluency."

Text_activity: typed response to the question "What were you doing while listening to the stories?"
